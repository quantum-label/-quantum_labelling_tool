About

Authoring

This tool is part of the QUANTUM project, specifically designed to create the data quality and utility label. The tool enables researchers and professionals to evaluate, label, and track the quality and usefulness of data across various stages of its lifecycle. By providing a structured framework for assessing data maturity, this tool supports informed decision-making, ensuring that data meets high standards of reliability and utility in line with the QUANTUM project’s mission to promote enhanced data management and integrity.

Funding

This work is funded through the QUANTUM project no HORIZON-HLTH-2023-TOOL-05-09 - Developing a Data Quality and Utility Label for the European Health Data Space.
